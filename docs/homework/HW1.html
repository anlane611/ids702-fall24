  1. (8 points) In 2023, 20% of a local bank’s credit card holders experienced fraud that forced them to cancel their cards. The bank expects to bring in 200 new customers in 2024. They want to know how many new cards they will have to cancel, assuming the probability of fraud is consistent for both years.

    a. Which probability distribution best fits this situation?

    b. How many customers should they expect to experience fraud?

    c. What is the probability that 50 customers will experience fraud?

    d. What is the probability that no more than 50 customers will experience fraud?
